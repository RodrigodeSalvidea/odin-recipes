Bacon and Eggs

Description:

Bacon and eggs are a timeless combination, having survived the dark days of dietary cholesterol demonization and coming back as reasonably complete, fairly nutritious breakfast to be enjoyed every once in a while.

Both eggs and bacon can be cooked in almost whatever way and still taste at least ok. I think over medium is the way to go for eggs, so that you can combine the runny yolk with the bacon and avoid any mucusy white, but anything goes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Strips of Bacon
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start cooking the bacon on cold pan. Cook until red both sides. Flip as need and then set aside
  2. Heat another oiled pan on medium high until oil is shimmering. Open eggs into pan and season to taste. Flip when solid underneath and then cook for about a minute. Serve while hot!
  3. Enjoy!
